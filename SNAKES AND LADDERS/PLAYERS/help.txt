DESCRIPTION.
Snakes and Ladders is an ancient Indian board game regarded as a classic.

HOW TO PLAY.
Once you press the START button it will direct you to the main menu/ home button.
You can choose how many players you want ranging from 1 player to 4 players, the avater, and the color of the avater.
Once this is done, click the in button and begin to row the dice.
The game can be paused, restarted and exited by clicking the pause symbol and the bottom right corner.

HOW TO TURN OFF SOUND.
To turn sound on and off you click then sound button.

HOW TO EXIT.
You can exit the game at any point during or after the game is over but chicking the pause button which gives the 
option EXIT.
